Exercise 1 · Daily BriefingResearcher · Critique

I start here every morning. Open Microsoft 365 Copilot at copilot.microsoft.com, click Researcher, keep the model picker on Auto, and paste the prompt below.

I want Researcher to read my inbox, calendar, Teams chats, and recent files, then hand me a structured briefing I can act on before the first meeting of the day.

Task 1.1🔍 Researcher · Auto (Critique)

Generate my GM daily briefing

Create my daily briefing from emails, messages, documents, and meetings so I can prepare for the current workday. I am Pak Rizky Wijaksono, GM Strategic Commercial & IT at Contoso Gold Mining Co. Prioritise commercial, operations, stakeholder, and IT signals that matter to me. Sections: 1. Opening paragraph: greet me like an executive assistant would, with the most important theme for today. 2. Key updates since yesterday: include overnight rig availability updates, any gold spot price move that matters, board pack items I should skim, JV partner emails from Nusantara-type counterparties, the upcoming Bupati courtesy call in Luwu Regency, and vendor renewal reminders. 3. Suggested tasks for today: show only items assigned to me or clearly needing my decision. Cross-check that the task is still open. 4. Unread email threads from the past week that likely need my reply: prioritise threads where I am on To/CC, @mentioned, or asked for a decision. Exclude automated messages. Show urgent or important items in a concise table and draft replies where useful. 5. Meeting prep for today: upcoming meetings only, with Meeting (time, title, organiser), Topic Overview, and Suggested Preparations. Rules: - Start with a header: Daily briefing, Day, Date. - Follow with a sub-header stating the timestamp of generation. - Start each section with a meaningful emoji. - Use only explicitly stated facts, no speculation or prediction. - If a section has no data, say that clearly. - Cite the source for material claims where available.
What success looks like: in about 90 seconds I have one screen with overnight signals, today's tasks, urgent unread threads, and meeting prep, all grounded on real tenant signals.
Bonus🔍 Researcher · follow-up

Drill into the single issue I must move today

After the briefing, I can ask Researcher for the deeper cut:

Take the highest-priority item from my daily briefing above. Pull together everything I have received on it in the last 14 days, including emails, chats, files, and meeting notes. Summarise the current state in 5 bullets, list the open decisions, and draft one short message I should send today to move it forward.

I use this after the morning brief. I want the Indonesian gold-mining sector scan, the peer benchmark, the strategic moves that matter in the next 12 months, and a one-page note I can take into the board discussion.

3.1 · Critique on Auto

Open Microsoft 365 Copilot → Researcher. Keep the model picker on Auto so Critique can draft, self-review, and return a source-grounded answer.

Task 3.1🔍 Researcher · Auto (Critique)

Scan the Indonesian gold-mining landscape

Research the Indonesian gold-mining sector for me. I am Pak Rizky Wijaksono, GM Strategic Commercial & IT at Contoso Gold Mining Co. Benchmark these peers: - PT Aneka Tambang (Antam) - PT Amman Mineral Internasional - Merdeka Copper Gold - J Resources Asia Pasifik - Bumi Resources Minerals - PT Freeport Indonesia Focus areas: - Gold price outlook for 2026-2028 - Ore grade benchmarks in g/t - All-in sustaining cost benchmarks - ESG posture - Community relations track record - Indonesian government royalty regime, including PKP2B and IUPK context where relevant - Local content rules - Downstream smelter policy For each peer, compare strategic focus, recent material moves, and the commercial or operating implications for Contoso Gold Mining Co. Cite every source. Flag any section where the evidence is thin.
Critique matters here because I want the citations checked before I act on the answer.

3.2 · Model Council

Switch the Researcher model picker to Model Council when the decision is too important for one model view.

Task 3.2🧑‍⚖️ Researcher · Model Council

Ask for the three highest-impact strategic moves

Model Council, given the peer benchmark above, what are the three highest-impact strategic moves Contoso Gold Mining Co should make in the next 12 months? For each move, give: - The rationale - The evidence, with citations - The main risk - One 90-day starter action Then show me where the models agree, where they disagree, and which recommendation is strongest on the evidence.
Why I use it: it surfaces disagreement instead of hiding it, which is exactly what I want before I commit capital or renegotiate partner expectations.
